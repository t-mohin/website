Mission Statement


For every push back, here is a little push forward

In a world, where being a women equals to being weaker, Made By A Woman aims to showcase the products designed and produced by women. The goal is to have all women regardless of age, race, and religion to understand that they are extraordinary even when society is telling them that they are not.
